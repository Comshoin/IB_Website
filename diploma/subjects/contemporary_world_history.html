Contemporary World History

Course Description

In the study of individuals and societies, this course, Contemporary World History IB SL Route 2, will focus on:

  1. Studying human experience, environment, and the development of institutions
  2. Identifying and analyzing theories about people
  3. Using data to test a hypotheses
  4. Appreciating the culture and diversity of other societies
  5. Embracing the idea of uncertainty and questioning ideas
  6. Promoting history as a discipline
  7. Encouraging understanding of the present through reflection on the past
  8. Understanding the impact of historical developments
  9. Becoming more aware of individual identity through the study of history

This Standard Level IB History course will address four major units: The Cold War, Authoritarian & Single Party States, Communism in Crisis, and Internal Assessment.

Major Learner Outcomes

  • Demonstrate knowledge and understanding of historical knowledge.
  • Apply and interpret historical evidence.
  • Evaluate and synthesize historical knowledge.
  • Use historical skills to produce structured writing.
