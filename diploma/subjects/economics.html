Economics

Course Description

IB Economics is a two year course which is divided into four section topics: microeconomics, macroeconomics, international economics, and development economics. Through the course, students are expected to develop an understanding of basic economic concepts and their application, understand the relationships among economic variables, interpret economic data and information, and develop an awareness and appreciation of current and global economic issues through a critical perspective. In addition, students will produce a portfolio of three commentaries on current economic issues to submit to the IB for their internal assessment.
