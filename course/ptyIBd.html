Selecting an IB Diploma Course of Studies at St Mary's

To complete a full IB Diploma, three courses must be completed at the Higher Level and three at the Standard Level (or four Higher Level and two Standard Level).

Higher Level Subjects

1. Language A1 2. Second Language 3. Individuals in Society
English Literature Japanese B Economics
Japanese French B Asian History
English Language and Literature #Other Language Information Technology (ITGS)
4. Sciences 5. Mathematics 6. Arts/Electives
Biology Mathematics Music
Chemistry Visual Arts
Physics
Design Technology

Standard Level Subjects

1. Language A1 2. Second Language 3. Individuals in Society
English Japanese B; Japanese AB Initio Economics
Japanese French B Contemporary World History*
English Language and Literature #Other Language Information Technology (ITGS)
4. Sciences 5. Mathematics 6. Arts/Electives
Biology Mathematics Music
Chemistry Mathematical Studies Visual Arts
Physics
Design Technology
# Other Language: Depending on availability

  1. One subject MUST be selected from each of Groups 1 through 5. You may choose a second Group 1 subject instead of a Group 2 subject. If you do not choose an Art or Music subject from Group 6, you must select another subject from Groups 1, 2, 3 or 4.
  2. All Higher Level subjects are for two years. All standard level subjects except History are also for two years
  3. *Contemporary World History Standard Level is completed in ONE year. Those students progressing to Higher Level will take the SL exam in Year 1, and the HL exam in Year 2.
