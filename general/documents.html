IB Self-Study

The IBO requires every authorized IB school to conduct a self-study every five years. For St Mary's, the process took place once again in the 2012-13 school year. The school evaluated itself in all aspects, and submitted a report (IB Self-Study Questionnaire) to the IBO. The IBO reacted by sending an evaluation of that self-study (IB Evaluation Report) that included recommendations. The evaluation also requested a response from us (St. Mary's IB Evaluation Report Response) which was submitted on October 4th, 2013. Also submitted was a timeline for the revision and completion of certain policies (Timeline for Completion of High School Written Policies). Please click on any of the links to view these documents.
